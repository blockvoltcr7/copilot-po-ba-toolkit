# 2.2 Notification Delivery Channels

## User Story
As a financial advisor, I want to receive risk alerts via email in addition to the dashboard, so that I am notified even when I am not actively logged into the platform.

## Acceptance Criteria
- **Given** a High severity risk alert is generated for one of my clients, **When** the notification service processes the alert, **Then** I receive an email within 5 minutes with client name, risk score, tolerance, drift amount, and a link to the dashboard.
- **Given** I have configured my notification preferences to "Dashboard Only", **When** a risk alert is generated, **Then** I receive a dashboard notification but no email.
- **Given** a batch of 20 alerts is generated in a single monitoring run, **When** the notification service processes them, **Then** I receive a single digest email summarizing all 20 alerts rather than 20 individual emails.

## Edge Cases
- Advisor email address is invalid or bounces — log delivery failure and retry once, then mark as undeliverable
- Email service is temporarily down — queue alerts and deliver when service recovers, with "delayed" flag
- Advisor is on PTO with a backup advisor configured — send alerts to both advisors
