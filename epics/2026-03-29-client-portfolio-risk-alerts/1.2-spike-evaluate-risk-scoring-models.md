# 1.2 Spike: Evaluate Risk Scoring Models

## Objective
Evaluate 2-3 risk scoring approaches and recommend the best model for calculating portfolio risk scores, considering accuracy, computational cost, and regulatory defensibility.

## Research Questions
1. Should we use a simple threshold-based model (asset allocation percentages vs targets) or a statistical model (VaR, standard deviation)?
2. What risk factors does FINRA Rule 2111 require us to consider for suitability monitoring?
3. Can we reuse any existing risk calculation logic from the current PortfolioView manual report tool?
