# 1.1 Real-Time Risk Calculation Service

## User Story
As a financial advisor, I want the system to automatically calculate portfolio risk scores for each of my clients' portfolios, so that risk drift is detected without me having to run manual reports.

## Acceptance Criteria
- **Given** a client has a portfolio with holdings, **When** the risk monitoring engine processes that portfolio, **Then** it calculates a risk score (1-10) based on asset allocation, concentration, and volatility metrics.
- **Given** a client's risk tolerance is set to "Moderate" (score 4-6), **When** their calculated portfolio risk score is 8, **Then** the system flags a risk drift of 2+ points above tolerance.
- **Given** a portfolio contains assets across multiple account types (IRA + individual), **When** the risk score is calculated, **Then** it considers the aggregate exposure across all accounts for that client.

## Edge Cases
- Portfolio with only cash holdings — risk score should be 1 (lowest risk)
- Client has no risk tolerance score set — flag portfolio as "Needs Risk Profiling" instead of generating a false alert
- Market data feed is delayed — use last known prices and annotate the risk score as "stale data"
