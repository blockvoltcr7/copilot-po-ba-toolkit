# Aha! Requirement: Real-Time Portfolio Risk Alerts

**Aha! ID**: WEALTH-2847
**Initiative**: Client Experience Modernization
**Requested by**: VP of Wealth Management
**Target PI**: PI 26.3

## Description

We need a way to alert financial advisors when their clients' portfolios drift outside of acceptable risk parameters. Right now advisors manually review portfolios on a periodic basis (usually quarterly) which means risk issues can go undetected for weeks or months.

The business wants some kind of automated monitoring that checks portfolio risk levels against the client's stated risk tolerance and alerts the advisor when there's a mismatch. This should work across all account types (individual, joint, trust, IRA, 401k rollovers).

We should also consider regulatory implications — FINRA has been increasing scrutiny on suitability monitoring and we need to demonstrate we're proactively managing this.

## Additional Notes from Business

- Advisors currently manage 200-400 client relationships each
- The compliance team also wants visibility into risk drift patterns across the firm
- Something about being able to configure alert thresholds per client segment would be nice
- We probably need this for the upcoming SEC examination cycle
- "It would be great if advisors could see this on their dashboard when they log in"
- Integration with our existing CRM would be a plus
- Mobile notifications at some point too

## Priority

High — regulatory driver + client experience improvement

## Acceptance (from Aha!)

"Advisors can proactively manage portfolio risk for their clients"
