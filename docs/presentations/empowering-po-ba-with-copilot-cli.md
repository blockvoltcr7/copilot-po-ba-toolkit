## Beyond Documents: The Agile Delivery Pipeline

Skills chain together into a complete Product Owner / Business Analyst workflow:

### The Pipeline

1. **Feature Brief Generator** — Turn vague Aha!/Jira requirements into structured, actionable feature briefs
2. **Epic Decomposer** — Break feature briefs into epics, user stories, and spikes with acceptance criteria
3. **Story Refiner** — Run INVEST checks, fix acceptance criteria, flag vague language, recommend splits
4. **PI Planning Workbook** — Generate pre-populated Excel workbooks with capacity, feature breakdown, and risk matrices
5. **PI Retro Report** — Transform raw retrospective notes into professional reports for leadership

### What This Means

- A stakeholder drops a messy requirement → You deliver sprint-ready stories with a PI plan in a single day
- No more spending entire sprints on planning artifacts
- Every output is consistent, professional, and ready for stakeholder review

> Speaker note: This is the full pipeline. Walk through the flow from messy stakeholder ask to sprint-ready backlog. Each step saves hours.
